COMP3104 DevOps course

DevOps courses provide essential skills for integrating software development 
and IT operations. Introductory classes cover core concepts like continuous
integration, automation, and infrastructure as code.

Benefits
Understand how open-source and cloud-specific DevOps tools help organizations transform product and service delivery with greater agility.
Develop a holistic DevOps strategy, roadmap and maturity model to support organizational change and digital transformation.
Enhance your career by gaining advanced DevOps skills needed by technology leaders.
