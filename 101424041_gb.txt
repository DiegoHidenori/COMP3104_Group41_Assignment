George Brown College 

Summary:
George Brown College is a public college in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. 
It offers a variety of programmes in different fields including business, 
technology, health sciences, hospitality and arts.

Campuses & Programs:
- St. James Campus: Business, Catering and Culinary Arts
- Casa Loma Campus: Programs, Construction, and Design
- Waterfront Campus: Health Sciences, Nursing and Dental Hygiene
