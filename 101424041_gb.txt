George Brown College 

Summary:
George Brown College is a public college in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. 
It offers a variety of programmes in different fields including business, 
technology, health sciences, hospitality and arts.

Campuses & Programs:
- St. James Campus: Business, Catering and Culinary Arts
- Casa Loma Campus: Programs, Construction, and Design
- Waterfront Campus: Health Sciences, Nursing and Dental Hygiene

For Student:
George Brown College focuses on hands-on learning. It has close 
ties to a variety of industries in Toronto, so many of its 
programmes include internships or co-op opportunities for students 
to gain real-world experience. For international students, the 
College offers English language programmes and a pathway for 
non-native English speakers.
