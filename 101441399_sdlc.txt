Importance of Learning DevOps in SDLC

DevOps combines development and operations  teams to enhance collaboration in the SDLC. It is vital for better software delivery.
Key Benefits:
1. Faster Time to Market: Automation speeds up releases.
2. Improved Collaboration: Better communication for early issue detection.
3. Enhanced Quality: Continuous integration reduces defects.
4. Increased Efficiency: Streamlined processes cut manual work.
5. Adaptability: Quick responses to market changes.
6. Higher Satisfaction: Frequent releases meet customer expectations.
Continuous delivery ensures that software is always ready for release.
Frequent feedback helps teams improve their products.
DevOps helps identify and mitigate risks early.
