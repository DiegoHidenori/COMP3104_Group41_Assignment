COMP3104-DevOps course 

Summary:
In this course, students will gain knowledge of DevOps techniques 
and use tools and technologies to deliver solutions such as continuous
Integration/delivery/deployment strategies, software development 
lifecycle, etc. 
Also, During the course, students will learn about the roles and 
responsibilities of engineers by conducting hands-on experiments 
in applying DevOps techniques, such as analyzing and using version 
control system workflows.
